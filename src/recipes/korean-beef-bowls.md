## Ingredients

- 2 lbs beef chuck roast, cubed
- 3 tbsp gochujang
- 2 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp honey

### Serving

- Cooked brown rice
- Sliced cucumber, kimchi, and sesame seeds for topping

## Instructions

1. Combine gochujang, coconut aminos, sesame oil, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and honey in a bowl. Mix well.
2. Add cubed beef to slow cooker and pour sauce over the top. Toss to coat.
3. Cook on low for 5-6 hours or high for 3 hours until beef is tender.
4. Shred or break apart the beef. Stir into the sauce.
5. Serve over brown rice. Top with sliced cucumber, kimchi, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.

## Notes

- **Soy-free:** Coconut aminos keeps it soy-free. Soy sauce works too if that's not a concern.
- **Spice level:** Gochujang varies by brand. Start with 2 tbsp if you're heat-sensitive.
