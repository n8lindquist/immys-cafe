## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 1 jar (16 oz) salsa verde
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1/2 yellow onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt

### Add Day-Of

- Juice of 1 lime
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 avocados, sliced
- Cooked rice or warm tortillas, for serving

## Instructions

1. Add chicken thighs, salsa verde, black beans, corn, diced onion, garlic, cumin, and salt to a slow cooker.
2. Cook on low for 6-7 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
3. Shred chicken directly in the slow cooker using two forks. Stir to combine with sauce.
4. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the top and stir in chopped cilantro.
5. Serve over rice or in warm tortillas. Top with sliced avocado.

## Notes

- **Pressure cooker:** 15 min high pressure from frozen, natural release 10 min.
- **Storage:** Freezer bag lays flat, good for up to 3 months.
- **Substitution:** Chicken breasts work but thighs stay more tender.
