## Ingredients

- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- 3 tbsp red curry paste
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 can (8 oz) bamboo shoots, drained
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar

### Add Day-Of

- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- Fresh basil and lime wedges
- Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

## Instructions

1. Combine coconut milk, red curry paste, bell pepper, bamboo shoots, fish sauce, and brown sugar in a slow cooker.
2. Cook on low for 3.5 hours or high for 2 hours.
3. Add shrimp in the last 20 minutes of cooking. Stir gently.
4. Serve over jasmine rice topped with fresh basil and a squeeze of lime.

## Notes

- **Don't overcook the shrimp** — they only need 15-20 minutes. Rubber shrimp is sad shrimp.
- **Heat level:** Adjust curry paste to taste. 3 tbsp is medium heat.
